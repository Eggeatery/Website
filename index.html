About us

We cook the best
Eggs

Welcome to our cozy egg restaurant! We started in 2017 because we saw a need for a clean, family-friendly place to enjoy eggs. Our restaurant is all about making eggs fun and delicious. We've got a menu full of tasty egg dishes for every meal. Since we started, we've grown a lot. Now we have restaurants in lots of places, both near and far. It makes us happy to see people enjoying our egg creations all over the world. So, if you're in the mood for some yummy eggs, come on in! We'd love to serve you some egg-cellent dishes and make your day egg-stra special.
